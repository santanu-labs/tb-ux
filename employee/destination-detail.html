About Goa

Goa, India's smallest state, is a tropical paradise on the western coast known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich Portuguese heritage. From the lively shores of Baga and Calangute to the serene beauty of Palolem, every beach has its own character.

Beyond the beaches, explore centuries-old churches in Old Goa (a UNESCO World Heritage Site), spice plantations in the hinterland, and bustling local markets. The fusion of Indian and Portuguese cultures creates a unique culinary and architectural landscape.
